Benefits of Chromebooks

  • Uses a cloud computing model and provides an easy way to access Google Apps for Education, which include: email, calendar, documents, data, ect.

  • These apps are also accessible from any computer that has internet access, all someone has to do to access these apps is log in with their school ID and password.



Cloud Computing

    Since all student documents used in the classroom are available on the cloud, there is no reason for the school's IT department to spend time backing up each student's documents, or spend money on maintaining an extensive data server. This is cost effective, and saves a lot of the staff's time (O'Donnell 4).
