  • Primary Keys: A unique value that represents a whole row. Every tuple (row) will be differentiated by the primary key.
  • Foreign Keys: This type of key exists in one table but refers to the primary key in another table. This is what allows us to link tables together.
  • Tuple vs. Attribute: Tuples is used to makes rows | Attribute are use for columns
  • SQL:stands for Structured Query Language. It is composed of short lines of codes that are grouped together.
